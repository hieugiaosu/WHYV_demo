Wanna Hear Your Voice: Multi-language Adaptable Approach in Target Speaker Extraction

The Hieu Pham, Phuong Thanh Tran Nguyen, Xuan Tho Nguyen, Tan Dat Nguyen, Duc Dung Nguyen

"In the sea of voices, I wanna hear yours."

Abstract: In audio clue-based target speaker extraction (TSE), research has largely focused on modeling the mixture and reference speech, achieving high performance in English where large datasets are available, while paying less attention to the consistent properties of human speech across languages. To address this gap, we introduce WhyV (Wanna Hear Your Voice), which tackles the challenge of transferring TSE models from one language to another without fine-tuning. The model achieves an SI-SDR of 17.3544 on clean English speech and 13.2032 on clean speech mixed with Wham! noise, outperforming all other models in transfer ability to different languages. The main code will be available at https://hieugiaosu.github.io/WhyV_demo

Instructions for Using the Playground
In the playground, you have two options:
  • Mixing Tool: Use this tool to mix two separate speech files. Select the appropriate SNR rate and press Mix Voices.
  • Upload: Alternatively, you can upload two separate speech files.
For the target speaker extractor, you can either:
  • From Mixing Tool: Use the mixed audio from the tool by selecting From Mixing Tool.
  • Upload Mixed Audio: Choose this option and upload a mixed audio file.
Don't forget to add 4-second reference audio by uploading it!
